# **App Name**: IB Unlocked

## Core Features:

- Homepage Navigation: Introduces the platform and guides users through interactive cards to either the 'Planned Student Flow' or 'Career Discovery Quiz'.
- IB Subject Selector: Allows students to choose IB subjects according to group structure and rules (HL/SL counts), with live validation and display of prerequisite checklists.
- Career Match Engine: Analyzes selected subject combinations against 27 career pathways, calculates match scores, ranks careers, and provides a 'Smart Adviser Tip'.
- Career Discovery Quiz: Guides undecided students by analyzing their interests to suggest top career pathways and recommended IB subject combinations that keep options open.
- University Search & Details: Enables students to select regions and view top universities per region for chosen careers, including entry requirements, typical IB scores, and official links.
- AI Academic Advisor Tool: An AI assistant providing clear and encouraging answers to student questions about subject choices for various careers, emulating a calm school counselor.
- User Plan Management: Provides optional user login to save, retrieve, compare (side-by-side), and export IB study plans as PDF reports.

## Style Guidelines:

- The visual design emphasizes a 'calm academic strategy lab' feel. The primary color is a Deep academic blue (#1F3A5F), with a hue of 213, chosen for its intellectual and trustworthy vibe in a light scheme.
- The background color is a Light neutral (#F8FAFC), which shares the primary's hue (210) but is heavily desaturated (20% saturation) and bright, ensuring a clean and supportive canvas for content.
- The accent color is a Soft teal (#3FBFBF), analogous to the primary color (hue 180 vs 213), chosen for its calming influence and contrast to guide user interaction. Functional colors like Success Green (#2ECC71) and Warning Amber (#F39C12) will be used for specific feedback.
- Headline (H1, H2) and Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its modern, neutral, and objective aesthetic, ensuring analytical readability and clarity across all text elements. Maintain 1.5x line height for enhanced readability, especially in body text.
- A foundational 8-point grid system ensures consistency. Generous spacing between sections will maintain a low cognitive load. Subject selection will utilize clear card layouts for an organized, structured feel.
- Subtle and reassuring motion, including 200–250ms fade transitions for page changes, soft highlighting on card hovers (subject, career), and progressive reveal for analytical results to manage information delivery.
- Simple, clean, and modern line icons will reinforce the analytical, structured, and trustworthy nature of the platform, aligning with the overall academic strategy lab aesthetic.
